Xˉi=1ni∑j=1niXi(j)，Xˉˉ=1n∑i=1k∑j=1niXij,n=n1+n2+⋯+nk {\bar{X}}_i=\frac{1}{n_i}\sum_{j=1}^{n_i}X_i^{(j)}， \bar{\bar{X}}=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{k}\sum_{j=1}^{n_i}{X_i^j},n=n_1+n_2+\cdots+n_k Xˉi​=ni​1​∑j=1ni​​Xi(j)​，Xˉˉ=n1​∑i=1k​∑j=1ni​​Xij​,n=n1​+n2​+⋯+nk​

F=MSAMSE=SSA/(k−1)SSE/(n−k)=∑i=1km(Xˉi−Xˉˉ)/(k−1)∑i=1k∑j=1m(Xij−Xˉi)2/(n−k)∼F(k−1,n−k) F =\frac{MSA}{MSE} =\frac{SSA/(k-1)}{SSE/(n-k)} =\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{k}{m(\bar{X}_i-\bar{\bar{X}})/(k-1)}}{\sum_{i=1}^{k}\sum_{j=1}^{m}{(X_{ij}-\bar{X}_i)^2/(n-k)}} \sim F(k-1,n-k) F=MSEMSA​=SSE/(n−k)SSA/(k−1)​=∑i=1k​∑j=1m​(Xij​−Xˉi​)2/(n−k)∑i=1k​m(Xˉi​−Xˉˉ)/(k−1)​∼F(k−1,n−k)

MSA：组内均方（mean square）
